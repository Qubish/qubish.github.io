Community Guidelines

  1. Respect Everyone's Time: We're all adults with responsibilities. Don't engage in pointless harassment or activities that waste others' limited gaming time.
  2. No Toxic Behavior: Keep communication civil and constructive. Banter is fine, but excessive insults, slurs, or harassment will not be tolerated.
  3. 4-Player Group Limit: Teams are strictly limited to 4 players maximum. Alliances between groups to circumvent this limit are prohibited.
  4. Balanced Raiding: Offline raiding is discouraged but allowed during designated raid windows. Complete base destruction (foundation wiping) is not permitted.
  5. No Blocking Resources: Don't wall off monuments or resource spawns. Everyone deserves fair access to the map's resources.
  6. Be Considerate With Building: Avoid excessive compounding or building structures specifically designed to lag the server. Remember, we all want smooth gameplay.
  7. No Exploits or Hacks: Play fairly and report bugs rather than exploiting them. Cheating of any kind results in an immediate permanent ban.
  8. Support New Players: Everyone was new once. Offer advice rather than mockery to newer players trying to learn.
  9. Join Our Discord: All players should join our Discord for announcements, updates, and community interaction.
  10. Have Fun Together: Remember this is a game meant to be enjoyed. Sometimes helping others creates a better experience than dominating them.

Our admin team enforces these guidelines with the understanding that we're all here to have fun in our limited free time. Moderators will issue warnings before taking action in most cases, but severe or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the server wipe schedules?
We wipe the server every other Thursday at 7PM EST. This schedule gives players two full weekends per wipe cycle, making it more accessible for those who primarily play on weekends. Blueprint wipes occur on the first Thursday of each month. All wipe announcements are posted in our Discord server with at least 3 days notice.
How active are admins if I need help?
Our admin team consists of working professionals across multiple time zones, ensuring coverage during peak evening hours (6PM-11PM) on weekdays and extended hours on weekends. Admin assistance can be requested through our Discord's #help-desk channel, with typical response times under 30 minutes during peak hours.
Is this server good for solo players?
Absolutely! We've designed the server with solo and duo players in mind. With our 4-player group limit, adjusted gather rates, and offline protection mechanics, solo players can make meaningful progress even with limited play time. We also have a dedicated #solo-lfg channel in our Discord where solo players can connect.
How does the offline raid protection work?
Our offline protection system increases the durability of building components by 50% when all team members are offline. Additionally, we limit raiding to specific windows (6PM-12AM on weekdays, 12PM-12AM on weekends) to ensure players have a chance to defend their bases when they're actually available to play.
Do you offer any starter kits or benefits?
Yes, all players receive a basic starter kit with essential tools and a small amount of resources to get going quickly. We understand your time is valuable! We also offer a slightly enhanced kit for returning players who were active in the previous wipe. VIP packages with additional quality-of-life benefits are available but carefully balanced to avoid pay-to-win advantages.
How do you handle players who break rules?
We use a warning system for minor infractions, giving players a chance to correct their behavior. More serious violations like cheating or extreme toxicity result in immediate temporary or permanent bans. All moderation actions are documented and transparent. Ban appeals can be submitted in our Discord and are reviewed by multiple admins to ensure fairness.
