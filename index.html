Welcome to RustyTea!

Life's busy, and Rust's a grind. We get it. RustyTea is a chill spot for mature players who balance work and play. Join us for relaxed sessions, teamwork, and a community that values your time.

Enjoy the crazy world we love without the pressure. We're here to make your limited Rust time enjoyable and rewarding.

Ready to experience Rust in a new light? Jump into our server and see what a difference a relaxed, mature community can make. We promise you won't regret giving us a try!

We are also new player friendly and Linux OS friendly!

Anti-Offline Raid System

We believe in encouraging online raids for a more engaging and fair gameplay experience. To achieve this, we've implemented an anti-offline raid system:

  • Double Protection: When a player logs off, their base receives 20% of the normal damage to the base and gradually returns to normal over the course of 48 hours. This encourages players to raid online and to avoid immediately raiding a player as soon as they log off.
  • Reduced Protection: For 48 hours there is some form of protection, damage or shoot the base with explosive to see if the base is protected!
  • Normal Raid Cost: After 48 hours of inactivity, the base returns to the normal raid cost.

We understand players love to raid, and offline raiding is a part of rust. This system promotes online raids and gives players a better chance to defend their bases while they're online but still allows offline!

Rules

  • Respectful Communication (Mostly): Trash talk is expected, but targeted harassment, real-life threats, or slurs are strictly prohibited. Keep global chat relatively civil.
  • No Exploiting or Cheating: Exploiting game glitches, using third-party cheats, or any unfair advantage is banned.
  • Raid Rules (Encouraging Online Raids):
    • Offline raiding is permitted, but excessive griefing is discouraged.
    • Building blocking or excessive land claim is not allowed.
    • Do not repeatedly raid the same small group of players.
    • The server features a plugin that reduces offline raiding damage to encourage online raids.
    • Raids that bypass the plugin's intent will be reviewed.
    • Abusing the raid plugin by logging off during an active online raid is prohibited.
  • Admin Discretion: Admins can act against disruptive/unfair behavior.
  • Reporting: Provide evidence to report violations. False reports are taken seriously.
  • New Player Tolerance: Show tolerance to new players.

FAQ

  • Q: Where do I find the Discord information? A: Check out below for server details.
  • Q: What time zone do you play in? A: We have members from various time zones.
  • Q: Is there a group size limit? A: There is no group size! Most of our players are solos, but we welcome groups! Alliances are plenty!
  • Q: Is there a minimum age requirement? A: There is no minimum age group, but keep in mind most of us are working adults who have little-no tolerance for immaturity.
  • Q: What is the server wipe schedule? A: Our server wipes biweekly, starting with the official force wipe on the first Thursday of a month. Blueprints will wipe monthly on the first Thursday.
  • Q: What is the map size? A: The map size is 3500-4500 depending on server population. We welcome suggestions and input on how its working out.
  • Q: Are there any custom maps or mods? A: See our list of plugins in our discord! We have considered custom maps, but have not done so. If you would like to see a custom map feel free to let us know!
  • Q: Is PVP always active? A: Yes, PVP is enabled, but we encourage fair fights.
  • Q: Do you have active admins? A: Yes, we have active admins who monitor the server and enforce the rules.
  • Q: What are the server connection details? A: Join the server by typing client.connect rust.rustytea.com in the f1 console, find us on the steam server browser, or under the modded tab on the in game browser!
