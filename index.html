About the Community

RustyTea is a friendly and competitive Rust server where players can build, raid, and survive together.

Our goal is to provide a balanced and enjoyable experience for all players, whether you're a solo survivor or part of a large team.

We are a community of players who enjoy a more laid-back Rust experience. We understand that many of us have daily jobs and responsibilities, so our server is designed to allow players to enjoy Rust without the pressure of being online all the time. Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned survivor, RustyTea offers a welcoming environment where you can play at your own pace.

We are also Linux player and new player friendly, with an active admin team dedicated to ensuring a smooth and fun experience for everyone. We strive to create a place where players of all skill levels feel comfortable and can grow together.

We hope to grow our community and welcome new players who share our vision of a fun, engaging, and fair Rust experience. Join us and be part of a group that values both competition and camaraderie!

Server Plugins & Features

Server Plugins

CarbonMod (Core Framework): CarbonMod is a powerful Rust server framework that enhances performance, modding capabilities, and customization options for server owners.

Advert Messages: Automates in-game announcements to keep players informed about rules, events, and updates.

Private Messages: Allows players to send direct messages to each other in-game using simple chat commands.

Sort Button: Adds a convenient Sort button to storage containers, letting players quickly organize their loot.

Skip Night Vote: Lets players vote to skip the night cycle, keeping gameplay active.

Time of Day: Allows server admins to control and customize the in-game day/night cycle.

Better Loot: Improves loot tables by balancing and modifying drop rates to enhance gameplay.

Stack Size Controller: Customizes stack sizes for different items, reducing clutter and improving inventory management.

Skins: Allows players to access and apply all official Rust skins to their items using an easy in-game command.

Sign Artist: Enables players to upload images onto in-game signs using image URLs.
