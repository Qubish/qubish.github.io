About Our Server

Welcome to RustyTea - where Rust gameplay meets friendly community! Founded in 2023, our server has grown into a tight-knit family of players who enjoy the perfect balance between PvP action and community building.

Our server is maintained by a dedicated team of admins who ensure a fair, fun, and drama-free environment. Whether you're a seasoned Rust veteran or just starting out, RustyTea offers something for everyone!

We run regular events, community projects, and friendly competitions to keep things fresh and exciting. Our custom plugins and mods enhance gameplay without taking away from the core Rust experience you know and love.

Server Rules

  • No Toxicity: Be respectful to all players. Trash talk is fine, but harassment, racism, and excessive toxicity will not be tolerated.
  • No Cheating: Any form of hacking, exploiting, or scripting will result in an immediate permanent ban.
  • No Griefing: Don't destroy bases just for the sake of it. Raid for loot, not to ruin someone's day.
  • No Base Camping: Don't camp outside someone's base for extended periods just to frustrate them.
  • Respect Safe Zones: PvP is not allowed in designated safe areas. These are for trading and socializing.
  • Report Issues: Use the discord to report problems instead of taking matters into your own hands.
  • Have Fun: Remember, it's just a game! We're all here to have a good time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often does the server wipe?
We do map wipes every Thursday and BP wipes on the first Thursday of each month.
How do I report a player breaking rules?
Join our Discord server and use the #report-player channel with screenshots/video evidence.
Are there any starter kits?
Yes! New players receive a basic starter kit with tools and a small amount of resources to get going.
Can I donate to support the server?
Absolutely! Check our Discord for donation links. Donors receive cosmetic perks but nothing that gives gameplay advantages.
What's the max team size allowed?
We allow teams up to 4 players. Larger groups will be asked to split up.
